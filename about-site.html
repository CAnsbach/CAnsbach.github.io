Overall Purpose of Website for Semester Project

This website will be a digital portfolio when it is finished. Projects that I have worked on througout my college career can be seen on this site. An overview of the projects that I have done is given in the "Projects Overview" webpage to give visitors a short summary of each project and a link to the its personal webpage. Other information about myself and my career will also be available on this site in their own webpages. Links to associated websites will also be available as needed in the asides of these webpages. Visitors will also be able to leave suggestions if they want to help me improve the site or help come up with ideas for projects. If visitors wish to contact me directly, a link is provided in the footer of each webpage to allow the visitors to email me.
